# Image Generation: Photorealistic & Product Photography

## Prompt 1 — Cinematic Scene

**Best with**: Midjourney, DALL-E 3, Flow/NanoBanana Pro, Stable Diffusion XL

```
rain-soaked tokyo street at night, neon signs reflecting off wet asphalt, a lone figure with an umbrella walking away from camera, shallow depth of field, anamorphic lens flare, blade runner color palette, shot on ARRI Alexa with Cooke anamorphic lenses, photorealistic, 16:9 aspect ratio
```

**Why it works**: Combining specific camera equipment (ARRI Alexa, Cooke lenses) with a defined color palette (blade runner) and technical terms (anamorphic, shallow DoF) gives the model very precise aesthetic targets. The lone figure adds narrative depth and scale.

**Key techniques**:
- **Camera + lens specification** grounds the image in real photography aesthetics
- **Color palette reference** (film, artist, mood) is more effective than listing individual colors
- **"Photorealistic"** at the end acts as a quality anchor
- **Aspect ratio** prevents awkward cropping

**Variations**:
- Swap location: "abandoned lighthouse on a rocky cliff during golden hour, volumetric fog"
- Swap mood: "vast white salt flat, gradient sky from deep blue to pale pink, minimalist"
- Swap gear: "shot on Hasselblad medium format, Kodak Portra 400" for warmer film look

## Prompt 2 — Product Photography

**Best with**: Midjourney, DALL-E 3, Flow/NanoBanana Pro

```
[PRODUCT] floating above a dark reflective surface, soft gradient lighting transitioning from [COLOR A] to [COLOR B], clean studio product photography, subtle lens flare from the key light, no background distractions, shot with a 100mm macro lens at f/2.8, apple keynote presentation style, 4:3 aspect ratio
```

**Why it works**: "Floating above reflective surface" creates an instantly premium look. The gradient lighting and macro lens spec add professional studio quality. "Apple keynote style" is a surprisingly effective shorthand for clean, minimal product presentation.

**Fill-in guide**:
- `[PRODUCT]`: matte black wireless headphones / glass perfume bottle / white sneaker
- `[COLOR A]` to `[COLOR B]`: purple to blue / warm amber to cool white / deep teal to black

**Variations**:
- Add "water droplets on the surface" for freshness/drinks
- Add "steam rising" for food/coffee products
- Replace "floating" with "placed on raw concrete slab" for brutalist aesthetic
